# /planning -- Planning Skill Group

Route user intent to the appropriate sub-skill:

- **Suggest next tasks** (user asks "what to do next?", needs task prioritization) -- read `references/suggest.md` and follow its instructions
- **Check milestones** (user asks "when is the deadline?", "are we on track?", wants milestone status) -- read `references/milestone.md` and follow its instructions
- **Capture progress** (user reports completing something, makes a decision, or changes project direction) -- read `references/progress.md` and follow its instructions
- **Status report** (user asks "project status?", "where are we?", wants a comprehensive overview) -- read `references/status.md` and follow its instructions
- **Analyze a decision** (user is weighing options, asks "should I do X or Y?") -- read `references/decision.md` and follow its instructions
- **Analyze experiment results** (user shares experiment results, asks "what do these results mean?") -- read `references/experiment-analyze.md` and follow its instructions

After completing the selected workflow, return control. Do NOT chain multiple workflows -- the chain system handles sequencing.
